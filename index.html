Learn about our journey, vision, and the dedicated team behind our mission to transform lives.

Our Story

Founded in 2008 by Dr. Amina Johnson, ILERICARE began as a small initiative to support orphans in Lagos. What started with just five children has grown into a nationwide movement that has touched the lives of over 5,000 vulnerable children.

Driven by compassion and a deep commitment to social justice, we've expanded our programs to include education, healthcare, shelter, and mentorship across Nigeria.

Today, ILERICARE Foundation stands as a beacon of hope, providing comprehensive support to children who have faced unimaginable challenges.

Our Impact Across Nigeria

ILERICARE Foundation currently operates in 12 states across Nigeria, with our flagship children's home in Lagos and satellite programs in Abuja, Port Harcourt, Kano, and Enugu.

Our community outreach programs bring essential services to remote areas, ensuring that even the most isolated children can access the support they need.

We partner with local governments, healthcare providers, and educational institutions to create sustainable solutions that address the root causes of child vulnerability.
